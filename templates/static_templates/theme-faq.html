Who can join Mehackit courses?

Our material is free to use for all curious minds. Our material is built for classes with a Mehackit instructor, but you are free to use these materials also outside the class room, with or without an instructor. If you are a teacher and wish to organize a Mehackit course, please contact us at hello at mehackit.org.
